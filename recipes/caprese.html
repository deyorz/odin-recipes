Caprese Salad

Experience the taste of summer with a classic Caprese Salad. This simple and refreshing dish features ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil, all drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and a touch of balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 400g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze, for drizzling
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into even slices.
  2. Assemble Salad: On a large serving platter, alternate slices of tomato and mozzarella. Tuck fresh basil leaves between each slice.
  3. Season: Drizzle the salad with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Finish: Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.
